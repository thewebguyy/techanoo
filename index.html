About Techano Solutions

From the Sesotho word, tekano, for “equity” and “equality”

Techano helps ambitious start-ups build software applications that are innovative, inclusive, and inspiring.

We are committed to creating and advocating for a world where technology not only brings us conveniences and helps us achieve our goals but also protects and empowers melanated and marginalized communities.

As a Design Justice Principles signatory, we are committed to creating and advocating for a world where technology does more than generate profits and conveniences. Techano aspires to leverage technology to create a more equitable, just, and joyous world.
